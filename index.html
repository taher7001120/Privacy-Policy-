Last Updated: March 17, 2025

FastPriv VPN does not log or store any data related to your online activity, including:

  • Any personal data related to your internet usage.

We believe in fully protecting your privacy, which is why we do not track or store any information that can identify your identity or online activity.

FastPriv VPN does not collect or store any personal data from users, such as:

We use strong encryption technologies to protect your internet connection and ensure your activity is not tracked. However, please note that FastPriv VPN cannot guarantee the security of data you share outside of the app (such as websites you visit).
